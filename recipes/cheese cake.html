cheese cake

Quick and easy Delicious cheesecake recipes

his cheesecake is smooth, creamy, and baked to perfectionon a buttery graham cracker crust.
It’s a classic dessert that combines richness with a light,melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Perfect for special occasions or just to treat yourself!

Ingredients

  • 200g graham crackers (or digestive biscuits), crushed
  • 100g melted butter
  • 600g cream cheese, softened
  • 200g sugar
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F).
  2. Mix the crushed graham crackers with melted butter, then press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth.
  4. Add the sour cream, vanilla extract, and lemon juice, and mix well.
  5. Beat in the eggs one at a time until fully combined.
  6. Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  7. Bake in the oven for about 50–60 minutes, until the center is just set.
  8. Turn off the oven, leave the cheesecake inside with the door slightly open for 1 hour (to prevent cracking).
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, before serving.
